About Banting

Here's some information about the Banting diet:


Principles of the Banting Diet:

  • Low Carbohydrate Intake:

    The Banting diet emphasizes reducing carbohydrate intake, particularly refined carbohydrates like sugar, bread, pasta, and other high-carb foods.

  • High Fat Intake:

    Instead of carbohydrates, the Banting diet encourages consuming healthy fats such as olive oil, coconut oil, avocados, nuts, and fatty fish.

  • Moderate Protein Intake:

    While protein is an essential component of the diet, the Banting diet recommends moderate rather than excessive protein consumption.

  • Focus on Real Foods:

    The Banting diet encourages eating whole, unprocessed foods, including plenty of vegetables, meats, fish, eggs, nuts, seeds, and healthy fats.

  • Avoidance of Processed Foods:

    Processed and refined foods, as well as those high in sugar and unhealthy fats, are generally avoided on the Banting diet.
